This initiative focused on consolidating inconsistent deployment patterns into a coherent internal platform model. The work balanced standardization with team autonomy and introduced clearer boundaries around runtime services, infrastructure ownership, and security controls.

## Outcome

The architecture approach improved release flow, reduced platform variance, and created a more reliable path for modernization without forcing a disruptive big-bang rewrite.
