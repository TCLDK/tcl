## Professional bio

I am Thanh Cong Le, a solution architect and technology leader with more than two decades of experience in software development, system architecture, embedded systems, IoT, cloud platforms, distributed systems, and agile delivery.

My background spans the full path from embedded devices and communication protocols to cloud-native services, data platforms, automation, and modern product delivery. That range has shaped how I work: architecture has to be clear enough to guide decisions, technical enough to survive implementation, and pragmatic enough to move real teams forward.

## What I do

I design and lead implementation of scalable smart home, IoT, embedded, cloud, and hybrid cloud architectures. My work includes defining architecture strategy, quality attributes, standards, governance models, and operational practices, while staying close to delivery through implementation guidance, stakeholder collaboration, and team enablement.

In recent roles at VELUX Electronics and Danfoss Electronics, I have worked on cloud-native and hybrid platforms, IoT modernization, infrastructure as code, CI/CD, data and algorithm capabilities, and embedded system redesign. I have also led and supported teams through agile ways of working, architecture alignment, and cross-functional delivery.

## Technical focus areas

- Smart home, IoT, embedded, and cloud system architecture
- Cloud-native and hybrid cloud solutions, especially on Azure
- Distributed architecture, serverless patterns, big data, and event-driven systems
- Data platforms including Apache Kafka, Kafka Streams, Azure Data Explorer, Azure SQL, MongoDB, and Cosmos DB
- Embedded and PC software development with C, C++, C#, Java, and JavaScript
- Modern UI and cloud application development, including React, .NET Core, and Azure Functions
- Secure connectivity, PKI, certificates, and software quality practices
- CI/CD, infrastructure as code, automation, Git, Docker, and package management

## Experience summary

My career started with hands-on engineering in embedded controllers, USB and TCP/IP protocols, refrigeration systems, PC tools, and product platforms. Over time, that expanded into senior engineering, project management, software architecture, cloud, data and AI architecture, and organizational architecture leadership.

I have introduced and applied Scrum and agile development across projects, led international and cross-organizational teams, coordinated delivery across locations, and helped organizations align architecture decisions with business goals, product constraints, and quality attributes.

## Education and learning

I hold a Master of IT in Software Construction from Aarhus University and a BSc in Engineering from the University of Southern Denmark. My continued learning has focused on software architecture, reliable software, distributed systems, Azure, cryptography, object-oriented analysis and design, design patterns, communication, and leadership.
